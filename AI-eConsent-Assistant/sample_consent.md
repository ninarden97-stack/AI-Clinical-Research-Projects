
# Sample Consent Excerpt (Example)

## Purpose of the study
You are being asked to take part in a research study because you may have a condition that the study medication is designed to treat. The purpose of this study is to learn more about the safety of the study drug and how well it works.

## What will happen if you take part
If you join, you will:
- Attend clinic visits over approximately 24 weeks
- Provide blood samples at multiple visits
- Complete questionnaires about symptoms and quality of life
- Undergo imaging scans as part of routine assessment (if applicable)

## Risks and discomforts
Possible risks include:
- Bruising, pain, or infection from blood draws
- Side effects from study medication, which may be mild or serious
- Loss of privacy if your data is not handled securely (we will take steps to protect your information)

## Voluntary participation and withdrawal
Your participation is voluntary. You can withdraw at any time without penalty or loss of benefits you are entitled to. If you withdraw, we may ask to continue collecting safety information already generated.

## Confidentiality
Your study records will be kept confidential to the extent allowed by law. Your data may be shared with regulators and ethics committees for review.

## Contact
If you have questions about the study, contact the study team using the official details provided in the full consent document.
