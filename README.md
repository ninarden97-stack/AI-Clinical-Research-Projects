# AI in Clinical Research – Exploratory Projects
Projects exploring how AI and data systems can support clinical research operations and regulatory processes.

## Overview
This repository contains exploratory projects examining how artificial intelligence and data systems can support clinical research operations, regulatory workflows, and evidence generation.

The goal is to explore practical applications of AI within highly regulated environments such as healthcare and life sciences.

The goal is to explore practical AI applications in highly regulated environments such as healthcare and life sciences.

## Background
With experience in clinical research, regulatory processes, and project management, I am exploring how emerging AI technologies could improve areas such as:

• clinical trial protocol review
• patient recruitment analysis
• regulatory documentation workflows
• data interpretation in evidence generation

## Projects

### AI Clinical Trial Protocol Analyzer
Concept: Use AI to extract key information from clinical trial protocols.

Potential outputs:

• endpoints
• inclusion/exclusion criteria
• risk factors
• regulatory considerations

Goal: Explore how AI could support faster protocol review and regulatory preparation.

---

### Clinical Trial Data Insights Dashboard
Using public datasets to explore patterns in:

• patient recruitment
• trial completion rates
• outcome measures

Goal: Demonstrate how data visualisation could support operational decision-making in clinical research.

---

### AI Governance Framework for Healthcare
Exploring responsible AI implementation in regulated environments.

Topics include:

• safety
• bias
• auditability
• regulatory oversight
