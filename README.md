# AI-Clinical-Research-Projects
Projects exploring how AI and data systems can support clinical research operations and regulatory processes.
## Overview
This repository contains exploratory projects examining how artificial intelligence and data systems can support clinical research operations and regulatory workflows.

The goal is to explore practical AI applications in highly regulated environments such as healthcare and life sciences.

## Background
With experience in clinical research, regulatory compliance, and project management, I am exploring how emerging AI technologies can support areas such as:

• clinical trial protocol review  
• regulatory documentation workflows  
• clinical data analysis  
• AI governance in healthcare  

## Projects

### AI Clinical Trial Protocol Analyzer
Concept: Use AI to extract structured insights from clinical trial protocols.

Potential outputs:
• endpoints
• eligibility criteria
• safety considerations
• regulatory risks

Goal: Explore how AI could assist clinical research teams with faster protocol review.

---

### Clinical Trial Data Insights Dashboard
Using public datasets to explore patterns in:

• patient recruitment  
• dropout rates  
• trial completion metrics  

Goal: Demonstrate how data analysis can support operational decision making in clinical research.

---

### AI Governance Framework for Healthcare
Exploring responsible AI implementation in regulated industries.

Topics include:

• safety
• bias
• transparency
• regulatory oversight
